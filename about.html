introduction

The Hill Top School is India’s top K-12 co-educational national day-cum-boarding school spread over 100 acres of lush green campus in Jamsedpur, Jharkhand (JHAR), India. The school was founded by the late Smt. Suneeta Singh on February 18, 1982. Drawing inspiration from Sri Aurobindo and The Mother, it undertook to work out and develop a scheme of integral education compatible with the contemporary needs. Since its inception, the students of our school have carved a niche in academics and sports. The outstanding performance of the school is borne out by the merit lists every year and various awards in academics, dramatics, music, dance, fine arts, and various indoor and outdoor sports.

The Hill Top is one of the leading boarding schools and is currently ranked 2nd in the country and 1st in Jamsedpur and Jharkhand (JHAR) in the category of co-ed day-cum-boarding school and 1st in Infrastructure in India by Education World. Hill Top School has been awarded the “Most Inspirational Secondary School” among deserving schools in India (Jury’s Choice Award) at India’s Top 50 Prestigious Jury Awards 2019 organised by EducationToday.co. We are selected in top Future 50 Schools in Shaping Success by Fortune Magazine for our last three years placements record second year as well. We have received the Best K-12 School Award for Global Collaborations by Business Magazine. We have received Certificate of Recognition for Best School by AFS. The Hill Top received Round Square Anniversary Challenge Award 2017 for Inventiveness. The Hill Top currently ranked 2nd in country and 1st in Jharkhand in day-cum-boarding school category in a survey conducted by EducationToday.co.

The Hill Top has been rated the Best in Academics, Infrastructure and nationalism by Brainfeed magazine. We have received jury award for the Exceptional Infrastructure of the year in 2017 and Green School of the Year in 2018 at Global Education Awards organized by Scoonews magazine. In 2017, National Education Excellence Awards organised by Mega Time 4 Media our school was awarded for the Most Promising National School in Central India. The school has an impressive infrastructure with dedicated staff to ensure that the boarding students get the complete advantage of being in a residential setting. The Hill Top is affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), an autonomous organization under the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India and Advanced Placement (AP) curriculum by the College Board. The streams offered are Science, Commerce and Humanities.

We offer our students international qualifications that are recognized by educational institutes and organizations across the globe. The school provides its’ students an opportunity to gain global exposure with its knowledge partners including Round Square, World Leading Schools Association (WLSA), Global Connections, AFS, British Council, International Award for Young People (IAYP) and International Association for College Admission Counselling (IACAC). Our goal through these programs is to prepare the students to get globally recognized.
